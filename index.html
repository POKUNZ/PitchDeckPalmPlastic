02 — Our Solution

We turn palm waste into biodegradable plastic pellets.

🌴

Collect EFB

Source from palm oil mills

Empty Fruit Bunches are collected directly from CPO mills — currently a waste they pay to dispose of, giving us near-zero raw material cost.
🧪

Extract Cellulose

Chemical acetylation

EFB contains 40–50% cellulose. We extract and acetylate it to produce cellulose acetate, the bioplastic base material.
⚗️

Blend Biopolymer

Add palm glycerol

Palm glycerol (also from palm waste) acts as a plasticizer, giving the material flexibility and durability comparable to conventional plastic.
🔩

Extrude & Pelletize

Ready-to-sell pellets

The blend is heated, extruded, and cut into uniform pellets. These are sold B2B to packaging manufacturers who use them in their existing machines.
