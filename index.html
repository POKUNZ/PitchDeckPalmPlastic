📋 Operation Manual

How to
Operate.

01
Place the machine on a stable, flat surface and connect to a suitable 3-phase 380–415V power source.
02
Verify all components are properly installed and secure. Check that the hopper, grinding unit, and extrusion section are locked in place.
03
Load dry palm EFB waste into the material hopper. Ensure moisture content is below threshold to prevent blockage.
04
Set the required temperature via the PLC touchscreen control panel. Turn on and allow the machine to reach operating temperature.
05
Monitor the process, observe the temperature gauge and pressure readings. Adjust settings via control panel as necessary.
06
Collect bioplastic pellets from the output conveyor into the pellet collection bin. Turn off and allow to cool after use.
🌡️ Operation Notes
  • ▪ Use only dry raw materials — moisture causes material blockage in the grinding unit.
  • ▪ Do not exceed machine capacity during continuous processing runs.
  • ▪ Monitor temperature regularly on the PLC control panel during operation.
  • ▪ Perform routine maintenance to ensure optimal pellet output quality.
⚠️ Safety Warnings
  • ▪ Do not touch hot surfaces during operation — heating chamber reaches 200°C+.
  • ▪ Do not insert hands into the machine while running.
  • ▪ Do not operate without proper personal protective equipment (PPE).
  • ▪ Keep machine away from flammable materials at all times.
  • ▪ Disconnect power before any cleaning or maintenance procedure.
🔧 Troubleshooting Guide
Machine does not start
Cause: Power not connected
→ Check the power cable and main switch
Material blockage
Cause: Wet or excessive raw material
→ Use dry EFB material and reduce input amount
Uneven pellet size
Cause: Incorrect temperature setting
→ Adjust temperature via PLC control panel
Machine overheating
Cause: Continuous overuse without rest
→ Turn off machine and allow adequate cooling time
